Finance Operations

Invoice & Billing
Approval Workflow

A clear and structured end to end workflow for reviewing, validating, approving, and processing vendor invoices, ensuring accuracy, compliance, and timely handling from initial submission through to final payment completion.

Total Steps
8
Approval Tiers
2
Threshold
$10k
Avg. Cycle Time
3–5 days
Process flow
📥
STEP 01
Invoice Submitted
Vendor submits an invoice via email, portal, or physical document. Details include vendor name, invoice number, itemised charges, and due date.
Vendor action Entry point
🔍
STEP 02
Accounts Payable Review
The AP team verifies vendor details, checks amounts against the purchase order, validates line items, and confirms receipt of goods or services.
AP Team PO Matching Verification
⬡ Decision — Errors found?
Yes — Return invoice to vendor for correction
No — Proceed to manager approval
⚠️
STEP 03 — if errors
Return to Vendor
Invoice is flagged and returned to the vendor with a written explanation of the discrepancy or missing information required for resubmission.
Error handling Vendor correction
✅
STEP 04
Manager Approval
The department manager reviews the invoice against the budget and approves or escalates. Standard authority applies to invoices under the $10,000 threshold.
Manager Budget check ≤ $10,000
⬡ Approval authority by amount
≤ $10,000
Department Manager
> $10,000
Finance Director sign-off required
🏦
STEP 05 — if > $10k
Finance Director Sign-off
For invoices exceeding the manager's threshold, the Finance Director performs an additional review and provides formal written authorisation before proceeding.
Finance Director Escalation path > $10,000
📅
STEP 06
Schedule Payment
The AP team assigns a payment due date based on vendor terms (Net 30, Net 60, etc.) and selects the appropriate payment method — bank transfer, ACH, or cheque.
AP Team Net terms Payment method
💸
STEP 07
Process Payment
Payment is initiated via the selected method. A confirmation reference number is generated and shared with the vendor as proof of payment processing.
Treasury Bank transfer / ACH Confirmation sent
🗂️
STEP 08
Record & Archive
The transaction is posted to the general ledger, the invoice is marked as paid, and all documents are archived digitally for audit trail and compliance purposes.
General ledger Audit trail Compliance
